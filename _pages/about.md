My research interests lie broadly in cryptography and related areas of theoretical computer science. More recently, I have become particularly interested in the quantum aspects of computation and cryptography, including quantum algorithms, quantum complexity, and quantum cryptographic protocols.

I enjoy formalizing intuitive, vague, or seemingly wild ideas into precise mathematical frameworks. If you think our interests align, I would be happy to hear from you.

I am currently on the academic job market and welcome inquiries regarding faculty, research, and postdoctoral opportunities.
